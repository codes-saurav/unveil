Empowering the Menstruators

Due to the stigma associated with menstruation and the shame it instills, in some cases, people fail to acknowledge their menstrual cycle. In addition to empathizing with all the women out there dealing with their periods, we want to include folks who do not identify as cisgender women but do experience periods. The initiative encourages empowerment for transgender people and women.

Join the Cause

Menstrual blood is the only source of blood that is not automatically produced. In modern society, this is the hidden blood, which is so seldom spoken of and almost never seen, except in private. Males are often excluded from participating in menstrual health education programs, which misses an important opportunity to tackle period stigma at an early age.

Spread a Word

Let's UNVEIL all the myths, stop the stigma, talk openly about periods, and manage them. Menstruation awareness emphasizes making hygiene products available to those who need them. Spread awareness,stay informed.
